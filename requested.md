This is basically a collection of ideas for things to do with wishes. It's part of quality of life, part something for unique playthroughs, part debug features, and part just something to generally mess around this. A lot of these features could probably be handled by different/already existing mods, but since they're all related to wishes I thought I'd group them all together.

Wish Completion Requirements
Change how many items are required to complete certain wishes (main the "get x amount of renewable resource" wishes). You could easily set this to 1 (or even 0) to heavily reduce the amount of farming you need to do, or even increase it if you want to for whatever reason. This could also apply to Dark Hearts, Mossberries, or even wishes where you otherwise need to collect all of a certain item to complete them (e.g. you can skip some pollips, great taste of pharloom ingredients, or complete infestation operation without the steel spines). I'd also throw the ability to change silver bell spawn chance in here since it doesn't quite fit anywhere else (you could also make some of the other drops rng if you want like old fine pins).

Wish Spawn Requirements
Manipulate the requirements certain wishes need to be accepted. Th easiest example is silk and soul, where you could lower/increase the number of quest points you need (or even how many points certain quests give), turn off any of the other requirements (fleatopia, mandatory wishes, faydown cloak, etc.), or even add new requirements (collect 5 silk skills or something idk). This also applies to other wishes, including act locked ones (Savage Beastfly 2, Fatal Resolve, etc.), wishes locked behind other wishes (donation locked wishes, broodmother needing 5 other songclave wishes, etc.), and any other requirements (like needing to see act 3 garmond/2nd sentinal a certain number of times before unlocking their respective wishes). You can also add extra requirements to other wishes as well (act 3 mossberies)

Accept Wishes Remotely
This would probably be something like a debug menu that lets you accept/complete/uncomplete any wish in the game at any point in time. So if you want to accept a wish without even having to go to it's normal location you can. You could also make wish boards contain wishes they don't normally have, which might be a better alternative than the debug menu thing (although you might not want every single wish in the game on every wish board at once, another thing you could customize)

All Wishes Mode
This could technically fall under the last category, but I like the idea of it being its own dedicated mode. Basically anything that only happens after accepting a certain wish (enemy drops, wandering/lost npcs appearing, certain bosses, etc.) will now always happen from a fresh save file even if the wish hasn't been accepted yet. There are probably some weird edge cases that could cause problems here (like SB2 being in the same arena as 4C, possibly needing to defeat pinstress before learning the needle strike, fighting 2 trobbios at once, etc.), so having an option to fix those would be nice (e.g. needing to defeat certain bosses before others appear, pinstress not disappearing before you learn needle strike, etc.). It might be fun to deal with those cursed scenarios anyways so having an option for either a "pure" version or "adjusted" version for this mode sounds like a good idea.

An UI
This is probably one of the bigger issues a mod like this would face (assuming this is its own mod and isn't added to an existing one). My best idea on how to handle this is to have a menu for toggling some of the option that will probably be more popular (only needing 1 enemy drop for some wishes, reduced/no silk and soul requirements, the all wishes mode suggested above, etc.), then there being some sort of in-game menu that allows you to toggle things on a wish-by-wish basis.
